About Me

Welcome to my world of possibilities

I am Noimot Abisola Balogun. a public health communication with an undergraduate in Dentistry, a Masters in Public health, and a PgD and MSc course completion in Mass communication, respectively. With interest in public health intervention design, mixed-method surveys research, and influence of social determinants; I am redefining health as a proximal leader who is reconnecting health systems to the public through people-centric messages and research dissemination.. As a career goal for 2022, I am seeking to broaden my horizon and gain international research experience and networking for health interventions through a PhD program in Health communication. I hope to use my added knowledge to champion an all inclusive medical training and research as well as a responsive health systems delivery in Nigeria, Africa and beyond.
